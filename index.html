Focus Areas

Three feedstock streams.
One integrated approach.

Deep operational expertise across the three primary organic waste feedstock categories driving US RNG project development today.

Feedstock 01

Food Waste

Source-separated organics, grocery and food processing residuals, and restaurant waste streams. Highest biogas yield per ton with strong tipping fee economics. Expertise in de-packaging systems, pre-treatment, and SSO contract structuring.

Feedstock 02

Agricultural

Livestock manure, crop residuals, and agricultural processing waste for farm-based digester projects. Deep experience with USDA REAP grant programs, co-digestion optimization, and rural utility interconnect requirements.

Feedstock 03

Landfill Gas

LFG collection system evaluation, gas upgrade to pipeline-quality biomethane, and wellfield optimization. EPA LMOP project development expertise and proven D3 RIN and LCFS pathway structuring for maximum credit value.
